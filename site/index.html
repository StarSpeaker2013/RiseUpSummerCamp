Activity Highlights

What Campers Will Do

Hands-on, screen-light, friendship-rich — designed for real-world growth.

⚡

Fitness Circuit

Relay races, mini soccer, Green Light game, and tug of war — build strength, teamwork & confidence.

🌿

Nature Scavenger Hunt

Discover plants, hidden objects, create nature art, and conquer team exploration challenges.

🧪

Engineering Challenges

Build the tallest tower, a recycled robot, a moving creature, or invent with Makey-Makey.

🎨

Arts & Crafts

Create your own Ocean World or Bunny Village — celebrate imagination & craftsmanship.

No-Fire Cooking Lab

Make fruit kabobs & Spam musubi while learning life skills and food safety.
