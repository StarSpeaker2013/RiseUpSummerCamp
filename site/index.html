5 Days · 5 Adventures

Daily Themes

Every day brings a new theme designed to build skills and spark joy.

Day 1

🌟 Team Building & Personal Growth

  • Welcome Circle & Icebreaker — Find Someone Who
  • Arts & Craft project: Ocean World or My Bunny Village
  • Foundations for the week ahead
Day 2

🌿 Outdoor Adventure & Discovery

  • ⚡ Fitness Circuit · Relay Race · Mini Soccer · Green Light · Tug of War
  • 🌿 Nature Scavenger Hunt — find plants, hidden objects, make nature art
  • 🎟️ Adventure Passport — earn stamps for completing activities
Day 3

🔬 Science + Engineering + Problem Solving

  • 🎯 Focus: Critical Thinking & Teamwork
  • Engineering: Tallest Tower · Recycled Robot · Moving Creature · Makey-Makey
  • Quiet corner · Social board games · Special Guest: Jindong Sha
Day 4

🍳 Cooking Arts & No-Fire Cooking Lab

  • 🎯 Focus: Life Skills · Leadership · Sensory · Creativity
  • Hands-on: Fruit Kabobs & Spam Musubi
  • Guest Speaker: Olivia
Day 5

✨ Magic, Showcase & Celebration

  • Magic workshop & performance prep
  • Camper showcase — share the week's creations
  • Awards, passport stamps & closing ceremony
Bonus

🎟️ Adventure Passport System

Complete activities → earn stamps → unlock rewards. A playful way to track progress and celebrate growth all week long.
